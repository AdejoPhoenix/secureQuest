Fintech companies hold highly sensitive financial data, process real-time payments, and are bound by strict regulations — making them an extremely high-value target. A single successful phishing attack can result in fraud, regulatory fines, and permanent reputational damage.

Regulatory Landscape

Training Isn't Optional —
It's a Legal Requirement

🏛️
FCA / PRA
Requires ongoing staff training on financial crime, fraud prevention, and operational resilience under SYSC rules.
🔒
PCI-DSS v4.0
Requirement 12.6 mandates a formal security awareness programme with regular training for all personnel handling cardholder data.
🇪🇺
GDPR / UK GDPR
Article 39 requires regular data protection training. Documented evidence of training is essential in any breach investigation.
☁️
SOC 2 / ISO 27001
Security awareness training is a mandatory control. Auditors require completion records and evidence of programme effectiveness.
SecureQuest provides exportable compliance reports — ready for FCA audits, PCI-DSS assessments, and ISO 27001 reviews.
